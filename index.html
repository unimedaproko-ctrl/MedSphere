UNIVERSITY OF MEDICAL SCIENCES, ONDO, ONDO STATE

OFFICE OF THE REGISTRAR

DIVISION OF ACADEMIC AFFAIRS

UNIMED/ACAD.A/CIR/30/SM/53

17th December, 2025

CIRCULAR TO ALL STUDENTS

Festive Holiday

The University Management has approved a festive holiday for all students of the University.

In view of the above, students are to proceed on festive holiday with effect from Friday, 19th December, 2025.

Students are expected to resume back to campus on Sunday, 4th January, 2025, and normal academic activities shall resume immediately upon resumption.

The University Management wishes all students a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year in advance. Students are advised to be of good conduct throughout the holiday period and to resume promptly as scheduled.

Thank you.

Mrs. Margaret O. OLAOLU

SAR, Academic Affairs

For: Ag. Registrar

CC: Vice-Chancellor
